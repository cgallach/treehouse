There are two kinds of weed dispensaries: medical dispensaries and recreational dispensaries.

At medical dispensaries in Canada, customers must have a valid prescription.

With a prescription, the patient can get their products dispensed to them as any other medication.

At a recreational dispensary however, anyone over the age of 19 in Ontario can actually purchase cannabis for personal use.

In the province, a maximum of 28g (one ounce) of cannabis can be purchased at any given time.
